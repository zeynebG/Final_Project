"Füyuzat"

"Füyuzat" journal, which was published on November 1, 1906 under the editor-in-chief of Ali Bey Huseynzade, but whose publication was stopped by the tsarist government in November 1907 due to the promotion of progressive and national ideas, 100 years later - in 2007, It was restored by MİMTA and started to be elegantly printed from the 33rd issue as a continuation of "Füyuzat".

"Işıq"

"İşıq-Qadın" journal is the successor of the historical "İşıq" newspaper which was active in 1911-1912. In 2012, It was restored as "İşıq-Qadın" journal with its founding by MİMTA.
