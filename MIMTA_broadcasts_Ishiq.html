"İşıq"

On January 22, 1911, the newspaper "İşıq-Qadın", which was published under the editor-in-chief of the enlightened intellectual Khadija Alibeyova and the publisher of Mustafa Bey Alibeyov, and which went down in history as the first women's press in our national press, ceased its activity after 68 issues - in 1912. , since January 2012, it was restored as "İşıq-Qadın" journal with the establishment of the National Heritage Cultural Historical Research Fund. This elegantly printed journal has won the deep sympathy of women with its articles on family upbringing, women's problems and a number of historical, cultural and moral issues. The editor-in-chief of the journal is Sadagat Kahramanova, deputy chairman of the State Committee on Family, Women and Children's Problems of the Republic of Azerbaijan.
