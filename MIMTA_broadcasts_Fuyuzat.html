"Füyuzat"

"Füyuzat" journal, which was published on November 1, 1906 under the editor-in-chief of Ali Bey Huseynzade, but whose publication was stopped by the tsarist government in November 1907 due to the promotion of progressive and national ideas, 100 years later - in 2007, It was restored by MİMTA and started to be elegantly printed from the 33rd issue as a continuation of "Füyuzat". Modern "Füyuzat" covers national-cultural, social-political, historical, moral-ethical topics. The journal is committed to national values, heritage, rich culture, and historical roots, and thus to be useful to our state and people. The chief editor of the journal is Mubariz Yunusov.
